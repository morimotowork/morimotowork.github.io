Art Collage (Beta/2019)

Idea generator website for creating collages. The user indicates what materials they have available to work with and the level of creativity they are at. From this, the website delivers a list combining random criteria for the composition.

A dynamic visual identity was also developed for the project. This work was part of my ongoing undergraduate studies at the Faculty of Fine Arts (U. Porto).
