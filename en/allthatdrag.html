All That Drag (2022)

Letterings for E! Brasil documentary All That Drag. Starring Don Valentim, Ginger Moon, Malayka SN, Natasha Princess and Penelopy Jean, the film portrays the value and plurality of drag art through the eyes of those who live in this universe every day.

Art direction by Beatriz Dórea and Isabela Vdd.
