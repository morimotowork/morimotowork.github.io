Virtual Landscapes (2020)

Versão em inglês
Series of prints and a personal diary to record some places where I have been during 2019. The images are originally photographs that were manipulated through algorithms, generating landscapes with particular details perceived from up close.

Each image has its own peculiarities, created by playing with code graphic experimentation and the personal meaning of each location. Memory and its virtual character are important concepts explored in the work. Something once real and subject to the human perception that shapes the reality of the physical world.

Part of the Future Vision exhibition of PCD21 @ Porto.
