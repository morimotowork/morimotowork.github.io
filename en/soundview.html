Sound View (Beta/2019)

Audiovisual sequencer that allows the user to combine samples in a timeline, executing associated sounds and images that are randomly displayed on the screen (position and size).

Sort of a real-time disc and video jockey. Some options enable greater control over the rhythm of the sequence as well as it allows to randomize the whole arrangement. This work was part of my ongoing undergraduate studies at the Faculty of Fine Arts (U. Porto).
