JoinType (Beta/2019)

Software designed to generate different shapes for the same letter by combining variations of the elements that compose the type. The variations of each element were pre-produced, separated between terminals, counters, stems and others, and when randomly combined by the program, they always generate a different typeface. One of the functionalities built into the program allows the generated types to be composed from the substitution of their pixels, by variations of the type itself.
